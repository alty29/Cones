Our Story

From my abuela's kitchen to yours

I grew up in Maracaibo watching my grandmother, mother, and father make empanadas. Now in Philly, I'm making them the same way—by hand, with the recipes they taught me.

Every empanada is made to order. No shortcuts, no mass production. Just good food made the way it should be.

Michelle "Michu" Medina Founder & Head Chef
