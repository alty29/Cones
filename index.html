The Story

Founded by Chef Michelle "Michu" Medina, ZULIA is a love letter to the flavors of Maracaibo, Venezuela.


From the kitchens of her childhood to the vibrant city of Philadelphia, Michu carries the secrets of three generations. Her cooking is a tribute to her father, mother, grandmother and great-grandmother, who taught her that an empanada is more than a meal—it's a memory.
